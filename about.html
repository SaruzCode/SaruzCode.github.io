About

The overall well-being of the residents of our city is the primary focus of the Kerala Cultural Center. Our goals are a reflection of our dedication to improving agricultural, social, cultural, health, and educational aspects of life. We plan and carry out a range of initiatives, such as: Emergency blood donation systems and medical camps. Health education and prophylactic initiatives in association with multiple organizations. programs that provide legal information via classes and seminars for underprivileged pupils. Activities that use libraries, reading rooms, and literary camps to advance literacy and cultural development. initiatives that support fight drug misuse. During natural disasters and rehabilitation initiatives are implemented. preservation and sharing of native knowledge and technology.

Mission

Our mission is to improve peoples quality of life by implementing all-encompassing programs that prioritize community development, health, education, and cultural enrichment.

Our commitments are to:

  • Increasing public awareness of and access to healthcare through health seminars, blood donation programs, and medical camps
  • Encouraging literacy and academic progress through reading rooms, libraries, and educational initiatives.
  • Promoting peace in society and raising awareness of the negative effects of substances like alcohol, drugs, and cigarettes.
  • Supporting initiatives for the welfare and rehabilitation of children during natural disasters.
  • Indigenous knowledge and technology being promoted and safeguarded for sustainable development
  • Promoting public involvement in development initiatives to guarantee community-driven advancement.
